Game Description

Overview

Bitcoin Price Runner is a thrilling game where you navigate a character through the historical price movements of Bitcoin. Survive drawdowns, collect bonuses, and experience the volatility of the Bitcoin market firsthand.

Score Mechanics

Price Movement

  • Score increases with positive price movements
  • Speed slider multiplies your score changes (leverage)

Jump and Fly

  • Base jump penalty: $150
  • Penalty scales with speed (e.g., 2x speed = $300 penalty)

Enemies

  • Orange-pill altcoin distractors to gain points
  • Orange-pill fiat maxis for higher points
  • Jump over drawdown bears to keep going

Events

  • Navigate through Bitcoin's history
  • Events can boost or reduce your score

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • ↑ (Up Arrow) or Click/Tap Screen: Jump
  • SPACE: Shoot Bitcoin Pills

Touch Controls

  • JUMP Button: Jump/Multi-jump
  • Orange Pill Button: Shoot Bitcoin Pills

Game Speed

  • Use the Speed Slider to adjust game speed (0.5x - 2.5x)
  • Higher speeds multiply both score changes and jump penalties!
  • Choose your strategy: high risk, high reward

Additional Controls

  • Start/Stop: Begin or end your game
  • Pause/Resume: Take a break when needed

Description of Game Characters

Player

Navigate through Bitcoin's price history events. Avoid bears and pill enemies.

Drawdown-Bear

Represents market drawdowns. Jump over these to avoid losing lives.

Fiat-Maxi

A tougher enemy. Defeat them for higher points and demonstrate Bitcoin's superiority.

Altcoin-Distractor

Basic enemy. Defeat them to gain points and clear misconceptions about Bitcoin.

Bitcoin Pill

Your weapon against enemies. Use it to orange-pill Altcoin and Fiat distractors.

Positive Bitcoin Event

Represents positive news or developments. Collect these for score boosts.

Negative Bitcoin Event

Represents negative news or FUD. Avoid these to prevent score penalties.
