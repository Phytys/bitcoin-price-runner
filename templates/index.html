Game Overview

What You Do

Run along Bitcoin's actual price chart from 2010 to 2025! Your score follows Bitcoin's price movements - gain points when it goes up, lose points when it goes down. Jump over market crashes, shoot down distractions, and collect real Bitcoin events to boost your score!
