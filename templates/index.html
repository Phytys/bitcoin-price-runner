Game Description

Bitcoin Price Runner is a thrilling game where you navigate a character through the historical price movements of Bitcoin. Survive drawdowns, collect bonuses, and experience the volatility of the Bitcoin market firsthand.

Score Mechanics

  • Score increases with positive price movements, multiplied by your chosen speed in speed-slider. (leverage)
  • Each jump costs a penalty of $100.
  • Orange-pill (shoot) Altcoin distractors to gain points.
  • Orange-pill (shoot) Fiat maxis to gain even more points.
  • Navigate through the the events in Bitcoin's history to gain or loose points.
  • Jump over Drawdown-Bears to keep your journey going.

Description of Game Characters

Player

Navigate through Bitcoin's price history events. Avoid bears and pill enemies.

Drawdown-Bear

Represents market drawdowns. Jump over these to avoid losing lives.

Fiat-Maxi

A tougher enemy. Defeat them for higher points and demonstrate Bitcoin's superiority.

Altcoin-Distractor

Basic enemy. Defeat them to gain points and clear misconceptions about Bitcoin.

Bitcoin Pill

Your weapon against enemies. Use it to orange-pill Altcoin and Fiat distractors.

Positive Bitcoin Event

Represents positive news or developments. Collect these for score boosts.

Negative Bitcoin Event

Represents negative news or FUD. Avoid these to prevent score penalties.
